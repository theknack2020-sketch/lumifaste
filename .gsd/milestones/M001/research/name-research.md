# App Name Research — Fasting App

**Date:** 2026-03-22
**Objective:** Find a name that is 100% unique on the App Store for an intermittent fasting app.
**Methodology:** Searched Apple App Store, Google Play Store, live domains (DNS + HTTP), and general web for trademark/brand conflicts.

---

## Summary

Of 13 candidate names tested, **most are already taken on the App Store or have significant conflicts**. Only **3 names** survived initial screening, and only **1 name — Lumifaste — is fully clear** across all three dimensions (App Store, trademark signals, domain availability).

## Competitive Landscape Note

The intermittent fasting app space is **extremely crowded** on the App Store. Major players include:
- **Zero** — the market leader (by Zero Longevity Science)
- **Fastic** — major competitor (by HealthVida GmbH)
- **Simple** — large player (simple.life)
- **Clear** — established app (Intermittent Fasting - Clear)

Most single-word and two-word compound names combining "fast/fasting" with common adjectives (pure, clear, simple, true, zen) are already taken or have significant conflicts.

## Important Caveats

1. **Formal trademark search required** — This research covers App Store presence, web presence, and domain availability. A formal USPTO/EUIPO trademark search should be conducted before final commitment.
2. **Domain availability should be confirmed** — DNS "no records" strongly suggests availability but doesn't guarantee it. Use a registrar to confirm before launch.
3. **App Store name reservation** — Apple does not reserve names. The name is secured only when you submit your app. Move quickly once decided.
4. **International trademark databases** — This search focused on US/English markets. Check local trademark databases for your target markets.
