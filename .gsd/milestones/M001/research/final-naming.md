## ✅ RECOMMENDED NAME: Lumifaste

### Why Lumifaste Wins

1. **Only candidate with .com available.** In a field of 13 names, only Lumifaste has an unclaimed .com domain. This is a decisive advantage — especially for a consumer health app that needs web presence for onboarding, marketing, and trust.

2. **Zero App Store conflicts.** No fasting app, no health app, no app at all uses "Lumifaste." The search space is completely open.

3. **Strong brand story.** "Lumi" (from Latin *lumen* = light) + "faste" (archaic/Nordic form of fasting) creates a narrative: *illuminated fasting, fasting toward clarity.* This positions the app as mindful and premium — distinct from the crowded "timer + calorie tracker" segment.

4. **International strength.** "Lumi" resonates across languages:
   - Latin/French/Italian: light, illumination
   - Finnish: snow (purity connotation)
   - No negative connotations found in English, Spanish, German, Portuguese, Turkish, Arabic, Japanese, Chinese, or Hindi.

5. **Premium aesthetic.** The trailing "-e" gives it a European/artisanal quality (like "Moleskine" or "Comme des Garçons") that elevates it above generic "XFast" / "FastX" competitors.

6. **Trademark defensibility.** As a coined compound word with no existing usage, "Lumifaste" has strong trademark potential. It's not descriptive (which would weaken a mark) — it's suggestive, the strongest category for consumer marks.

### Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Users type "Lumifast" (no -e) | Register lumifast.com as a redirect. Register both app names if possible. |
| "Lumi" prefix confusion with Lumy (sun tracker) | Different category (fasting vs. photography). Apple's algorithm distinguishes. |
| Pronunciation ambiguity | Standard: **LOO-mee-fast**. Marketing should reinforce this early. |
