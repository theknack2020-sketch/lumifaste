# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 22, 2026**

## Overview

Lumifaste ("the App") is developed by The Knack. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored on Your Device Only
- Fasting session history (start time, end time, duration, plan type)
- App settings and preferences
- Onboarding selections (fasting goal, preferred plan)

This data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. **We never transmit your fasting data to any server.**

### Data Synced via iCloud (Optional)
If you have iCloud enabled, your fasting data may sync between your Apple devices via Apple's CloudKit. This data is encrypted and managed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

### Analytics (Anonymous)
We use TelemetryDeck for anonymous, privacy-first analytics. TelemetryDeck does not collect personal data, does not use advertising identifiers, and is fully GDPR compliant. We collect:
- App usage patterns (which features are used, session duration)
- Device type and iOS version (anonymized)
- Crash reports

We do **not** collect: names, email addresses, location data, health data, photos, contacts, or any personally identifiable information.

### HealthKit Data
If you grant permission, the App may read weight and activity data from Apple Health. This data is:
- Read only when you open the App
- Never stored on our servers
- Never shared with third parties
- Never used for advertising
- Processed entirely on your device

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Photos or media
- Contacts
- Browsing history
- Advertising identifiers (no IDFA)

## Advertising
Lumifaste contains **zero advertisements**. We do not integrate any ad networks, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs.

## Third-Party Services
- **Apple (CloudKit)**: Optional device sync. See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).
- **TelemetryDeck**: Anonymous analytics. See [TelemetryDeck's Privacy Policy](https://telemetrydeck.com/privacy/).

## Data Retention
Your data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data. We retain no server-side copies.

## Your Rights
- **Access**: Your data is on your device — you have full access at all times.
- **Deletion**: Delete the App to remove all local data. For iCloud data, manage it in Settings > Apple ID > iCloud.
- **Portability**: Future versions will include data export functionality.

## Children's Privacy
Lumifaste is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App and on our website.
