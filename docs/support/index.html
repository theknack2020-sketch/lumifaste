Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a fast?

Open the app, select your fasting plan (16:8, 18:6, etc.) from the chips at the bottom, then tap "Start Fast." The circular timer will begin tracking your progress.

What are fasting stages?

As your fast progresses, your body goes through different metabolic states: Fed → Early Fasting → Fat Burning → Ketosis → Autophagy. The app shows which stage you're in based on scientific research.

Is the app really ad-free?

Yes, 100%. Lumifaste will never show you any advertisements. This is a core promise of the app. We make money through optional Premium subscriptions, not ads.

What's included in the free version?

The fasting timer, all fasting plans, milestone notifications, and your last 7 fasting sessions are completely free. Forever. No tricks.

What does Premium include?

Premium unlocks unlimited fasting history, advanced trend analysis, and detailed fasting stage insights. You can try it free for 7 days.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings → tap "Restore Purchases." This will restore your Premium subscription if you've previously purchased one.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Lumifaste → Cancel. You'll keep Premium access until the end of your billing period.
